Overview

KPIs are a scorecard for company health. You only need a handful of KPIs to monitor your company's vital signs. Only measure what you want to move so you can put energy where you want to effect change. We’ve found that it is important to measure a few KPIs in each of 4 categories: Employees, Customers, Processes, and Revenue. These fall under the disciplines of human resources, customer satisfaction, business processes, business strategy and many more. First make sure you choose the right KPIs for your business.
